Something interesting about me is that I play the tuba. I started in 5th grade and have played ever since. The photo above shows me performing at a Tuba Christmas event, which is a Christmas concert consisting only of tubas and euphoniums. I currently play the only tuba in the University of St. Thomas Symphonic Wind Ensemble and have been a member since last year.

The other photo is of my friends and me. I included it because I spend most of my free time with them, outside of class or rehearsals. Recently we've developed a bit of a Mario Kart addiction and have had a lot of fun playing together.
